About Greek Gods

This is a community-driven site for maintaining information on the family tree of Greek gods and other Greek mythological figures. This site is for Homer's account of the family tree, according to the Iliad and Odyssey.

How it works

Click "search" to search for specific subjects. To contribute to the site, either log in or sign up. Then either click the "edit" button on a subject's page to edit their information, or go to "add subject" from your profile to add a new subject. To see your contribution history, visit your profile page.
